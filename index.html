How we met

Andrew and Emma met online on Instagram through a mutual Catholic account they both followed. That Catholic account had posted a dating post for Catholic people to meet each other, and Andrew had posted a short bio about himself as a comment to this post around December 2023. Emma wasn't able to respond to any of the comments until she turned 18 in March 2024, so on her birthday, March 10, 2024, was when Emma reached out to Andrew on Instagram. They began talking and doing video calls as often as they could, often stretching into the early hours of the morning - after all, they were college students, and who needs sleep anyway. Their first date was on Sunday, March 24 when Andrew made the 4 hour drive from Indiana to her house in Michigan where they attended Mass with her family and spent the day walking around the city of Wyandotte, visiting other churches, talking about everything.

Event

8th August

Wedding Mass 11AM - 12:30AM

As marriage is a Sacrament, this is the main event and the most important part where the marriage happens! The Wedding Mass will be done in the Tridentine Rite (Traditional Latin Mass) at St. Stephen Church. This Mass consists of two parts: the marriage service, and the Nuptial Mass. The exchange of vows and blessing from the priest occurs prior to the Mass during the marriage service, and the Nuptial Mass follows where the bride and groom participate in their first Eucharist as husband and wife.
